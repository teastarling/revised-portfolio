About Me

After spending the last 10 years in New York City working with everything from snow leopards to sea lions, this Georgia girl has come home to do what it takes to chase the dream of becoming a Software Developer. My aim is to find the simplest path to reach the highest user satisfaction possible for each project while meeting client requisites. I am eager to apply my skills to new challenges as a team member within a fast-paced environment in order to reach both personal and company-wide goals.
